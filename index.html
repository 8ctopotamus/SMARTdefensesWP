PERSONAL PROTECTION DEVICES

Four Point Protection Plan

Because life is so important, I have devised a four step (A, B, C and D) protection plan that maximizes possibilities for your protection and safety. The lack of a reliable protection plan in hopes of being safe is not sufficient because once a life is lost, you cannot get it back. Death is final.

Step A - HAVE AND CARRY A GUN

A gun is the most powerful protection device. It has great power to stop an attacker at a distance; thus keeping you safe. The distance is important because it is best to avoid physical contact with an attacker. If you get a gun, make sure you are knowledgeable on how to use it. This includes knowing legal issues related to self-defense. If you do not want a gun, Step B becomes extremely important.

Step B - PEPPER SPRAY

Like a gun, pepper spray can stop an attacker before the attacker reaches you. This allows you to avoid physical contact with an attacker which reduces the chance of severe injury, rape or loss of life. There are specific best techniques for successfully using pepper spray. Be sure to learn them from a qualified trainer.

Know that power and effectiveness of a pepper spray is greatly reduced after the first few sprays. Also, your pepper spray should be replaced every 2 years because over time its potency diminishes.
